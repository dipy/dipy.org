=======================================================
Community Spotlight: New Collaboration Proposals
=======================================================

:date: 2024-11-04
:author: Matthew Brett
:category: Community

The DIPY and Nipy communities are always open to new contributors! Recently, Oscar Prata, a neuropsychologist and computer programmer, reached out to the neuroimaging mailing list to propose a collaboration on designing and developing a new website.

While the request initially sparked a brief discussion on listserve security, it quickly turned into a warm welcome from core maintainers.

Community Response
==================
Matthew Brett welcomed the suggestion, noting the importance of community-led improvements. Christopher Markiewicz also highlighted that those interested in helping with the `nipy.org <https://nipy.org>`_ website or specific project documentations should feel free to open issues on GitHub.

How to Collaborate
==================
If you are a developer, researcher, or designer looking to help:
* **Propose Ideas:** Open an issue on the `nipy.github.io repository <https://github.com/nipy/nipy.github.io/issues>`_.
* **Join the Discussion:** Stay active on the neuroimaging mailing list to see where help is needed most.

We look forward to seeing how these community collaborations shape the future of our tools!
