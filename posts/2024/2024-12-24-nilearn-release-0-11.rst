====================================
Nilearn Releases: 0.11.0 and 0.11.1
====================================

:date: 2024-12-24
:author: Himanshu Aggarwal
:category: Community

Merry Christmas! The Nilearn team has released versions 0.11.0 and 0.11.1.

This update introduces a major new feature: a **new surface API** that allows users to represent surface meshes and data directly within Nilearn. This is a significant step forward for surface-based brain imaging analysis.

Key Highlights:
===============
* **New Surface API:** Enhanced representation for meshes and surface data.
* **Bug Fixes:** Version 0.11.1 addresses initial bugs found in the 0.11.0 release.

For more details, check out the official announcement on `Neurostars <https://neurostars.org/t/nilearn-releases-0-11-0-and-0-11-1/31278>`_.
