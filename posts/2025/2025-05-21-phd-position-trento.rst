Open PhD Position: Brain Connectivity and Artificial Intelligence
=====================================================================

:date: 2025-05-21
:author: Paolo Avesani
:category: Community

The Neuroinformatics Laboratory at Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK), in collaboration with the University of Trento, Italy, is offering a 4-year PhD scholarship.

Research Focus
==============
The project aims to develop AI-based solutions (Foundation Models) to model inter-individual variability in brain connectivity, bypassing canonical preprocessing strategies. The goal is to capture structural and functional aspects of brain organization to improve clinical translation for mental health conditions.

Key Details
===========
* **Institution:** University of Trento & Fondazione Bruno Kessler (Trento, Italy).
* **Deadline for Application:** June 5, 2025.
* **Start Date:** November 1, 2025.
* **Contact:** Ludovico Coletta (lcoletta@fbk.eu)

Requirements
============
The successful candidate should have an MSc in computer science, biomedical engineering, physics, or any STEM discipline. Experience in neuroimaging data analysis and geometric deep learning is highly valued.

How to Apply
============
Detailed information and the application portal can be found at the `University of Trento Admission Page <https://phd.unitn.it/drcimec/116/admission-doctorate-cognitive-and-brain-sciences>`_.
