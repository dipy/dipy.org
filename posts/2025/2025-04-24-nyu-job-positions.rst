Job Openings: Lab Associate and Research Scientist at NYU CBI
===============================================================

:date: 2025-04-24
:author: Chrysa Papadaniil
:category: Community

The Center for Brain Imaging (CBI) at New York University has announced two open positions for dedicated professionals to join their team.

Available Positions
===================

1. Laboratory Associate
-----------------------
This full-time role focuses on the efficient and safe operation of MRI scanner equipment, overseeing researcher training, and maintaining safety protocols. 

* **Requirements:** Bachelor's degree in a science-related field, 2+ years post-BA/BS experience, and familiarity with magnetic resonance equipment.
* **Apply here:** `https://apply.interfolio.com/166760 <https://apply.interfolio.com/166760>`_

2. Research Scientist
---------------------
This role involves overseeing MRI scanning technology, managing hardware/software troubleshooting, and contributing to extramural research funding efforts.

* **Requirements:** PhD in a science-related field (minimum 1 year post-PhD), experience with MRI, scientific writing, and data modeling skills.
* **Apply here:** `https://apply.interfolio.com/166773 <https://apply.interfolio.com/166773>`_

About NYU CBI
=============
The Center for Brain Imaging at NYU is at the forefront of neuroimaging research. For informal inquiries, you can reach out to Chrysa Papadaniil (chrysa@nyu.edu) or cbi-info@nyu.edu.
